OASES Restaurant

Design Build, New York, United States

Where design precision meets spatial serenity. A refined harmony of structure and soul — created by MOKHA.

The Narrative

A space that speaks through proportion and precision. OASES was envisioned as a serene retreat where light, form, and material converse in quiet elegance. Every detail — from structure to finish — follows MOKHA’s pursuit of functional beauty and efficiency.

OASES embodies the dual nature of design: intuition and logic. The architecture captures the warmth of human touch within a framework of technical mastery. Light unfolds softly across sculpted geometry, evoking both calm and clarity. Through simplicity and precision, MOKHA defines elegance as quiet confidence.
