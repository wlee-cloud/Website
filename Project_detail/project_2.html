Seoul Gallery

Arts & Culture — Seoul, Korea

A space where architecture and art converge in harmony. MOKHA envisioned Seoul Gallery as an architectural expression of balance — between light and shadow, openness and intimacy, structure and stillness.

The Narrative

Seoul Gallery is conceived as a meditative sequence of spatial experiences — a journey through light, texture, and proportion. The minimalist composition removes excess, allowing the dialogue between natural light and material to define the space.

The building’s linear geometry and restrained palette create quiet drama. Floating planes and framed apertures evoke a sense of rhythm and reflection, while maintaining a timeless architectural clarity.

Designed not to dominate, but to accompany art, Seoul Gallery stands as an embodiment of MOKHA’s philosophy: silence as luxury, and precision as poetry.
