PARK AVENUE OFFICE

About the Project

Located in the heart of Manhattan, the Park Avenue Office project redefines the modern commercial interior. MOKHA collaborated closely with the client to transform a traditional office space into a refined, light-filled environment that encourages creativity, efficiency, and connection.

Natural tones, textured walls, and warm lighting blend sophistication with comfort. Custom-built furniture and seamless details express the firm's design philosophy: precision, timelessness, and authenticity.

The open floor plan maximizes spatial efficiency while maintaining a sense of calm. Curved plaster partitions create visual flow between workstations and lounges, with carefully placed greenery introducing warmth into the space. Sustainable materials and high acoustic insulation ensure both comfort and durability. The result is a workspace that reflects both MOKHA’s commitment to craft and the client’s focus on innovation and well-being.
