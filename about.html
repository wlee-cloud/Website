Our Story

Founded in Manhattan, New York, MOKHA is a full-service Design & Build firm redefining modern architecture and interior construction. Our philosophy is rooted in precision, sustainability, and a deep respect for design integrity.

Thoughtful Design. Purposeful Execution.

At MOKHA, every project begins with a story — a vision shaped by collaboration and curiosity. We blend architectural clarity with construction expertise to deliver spaces that endure and inspire. Our multidisciplinary approach unites architects, engineers, and builders under one seamless process, ensuring that every detail reflects our clients’ identity and our commitment to excellence.

Our work spans commercial, hospitality, and cultural environments — designed not only to function beautifully, but to resonate emotionally. We believe good design is timeless, and thoughtful construction is what brings it to life.
